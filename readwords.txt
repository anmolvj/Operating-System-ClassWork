The Rinnegan was first manifested by Hagoromo Ōtsutsuki, derived from the Rinne Sharingan wielded by his mother. It is suggested his Rinnegan was a kekkei mōra,[Notes 1] and with it, he saved the world from the Ten-Tails and created ninshū, the precursor to modern ninjutsu.
