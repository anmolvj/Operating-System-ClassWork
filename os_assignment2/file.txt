The series follows a group of rich, privileged, and elite high school students as they 
are groomed to take over their families' business empires. These wealthy students seem to 
have everything under control, except their love lives. Kim Tan (Lee Min-ho) is a handsome 
and wealthy heir to a large Korean conglomerate who is studying in the U.S.[6] Meanwhile, his 
brother Kim Won (Choi Jin-hyuk) tries to take control of the family business.[7] While in the 
States, Kim Tan meets Cha Eun-sang (Park Shin-hye).[8] But Kim Tan is engaged to Rachel Yoo (Kim Ji-won). 
Rachel's future stepbrother and Kim Tan's former best friend, Choi Young-do (Kim Woo-bin) (who also attends
 the same school as Kim Tan and Eun-sang) starts falling for Eun-sang as well. Troubles arise as the heirs 
realize the differences between money and the real world and they soon learn what love is.
